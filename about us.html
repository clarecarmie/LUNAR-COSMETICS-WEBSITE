The Making of LUNAR

🌙🌼Our Story🌼🌙

LUNAR was born on a quiet, meaningful night — a night that captured the heart of an acne fighter who has long struggled to feel beautiful. In moments of sadness and self-doubt, I would often look up at the moon. Though its surface is far from smooth, it continues to shine brightly, illuminating even the darkest skies. It reminded me that there is strength in vulnerability, and beauty in imperfection.

The floral element of the brand was inspired by the tuberose flower (harum sundal malam), admired for its enchanting fragrance and timeless elegance. My fascination with this flower began with my all-time favorite film, Pontianak Harum Sundal Malam, where Maya Karin’s haunting beauty left a lasting impression on me. It sparked a deep appreciation for mysterious, graceful femininity.

LUNAR is more than a beauty brand — it is a reflection of my journey, and a tribute to every woman who has ever struggled with her skin, her confidence, or her worth. It is a celebration of quiet resilience, inner radiance, and the power of embracing who we are — flaws and all.

🌙✨LUNAR Brand Timeline✨🌙

✧ 2021 – The Idea Was Born

LUNAR began with a clear vision: a beauty brand that uplifts confidence, speaks to the soul, and celebrates grace — inspired by the night sky and blooming flowers of the dark.

✧ 2022 – Laying the Foundation

Brand development began: skin-friendly ingredients, product testing, and a visual identity in navy, gold, and floral elegance.

✧ 2023 – Skill & Service Development

LUNAR introduced bridal, event, and personal makeup services — elevating natural beauty with soulful artistry.

✧ 2024 – Official Brand Launch

Public debut with:
- Makeup Services
- Lip Gloss, Foundation, Contour Stick & Blusher — crafted in moonlight tones.

✧ 2025 – Recognition & Growth

Collaborations, magazine features & creative campaigns made LUNAR a beauty name known for heart and elegance.

✧ 2026 – The First LUNAR Studio

Plans begin for a flagship studio: private consultations, exclusive launches, and a soulful beauty sanctuary.

✧ 2027 & Beyond – Expanding with Grace

-Upcoming: Lip Mattes, Eyeshadow Palettes, Setting Sprays
-Service growth locally & internationally
-LUNAR evolves with elegance and soul.
